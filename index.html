I am an audio engineer, video editor and musician. I was born and raised in likely one of the most fantastic areas of the world, the Pacific Northwest, and currently based out of Seattle. I studied Community Arts & Music with a minor in Audio Engineering & Society at Fairhaven College in Bellingham, WA and graduated in 2015. Since moving to Seattle in 2018, I have been working on recordings with my on-the-go mobile recording studio Deadpan Audio as well as a number of professional recording studios in the area. I also take on video productions of varying sizes on a project-by-project basis. Get in touch, I would love to be a part of your next project!
