I am an audio engineer, video editor and musician. I was born and raised in likely one of the most fantastic areas of the world, the Pacific Northwest, and currently based out of Seattle. I studied Community Arts & Music with a minor in Audio Engineering & Society at Fairhaven College in Bellingham, WA. I work at 7 Hills Studio and my on-the-go mobile recording setup called Deadpan Audio. Get in touch, I would love to be a part of your next project!
